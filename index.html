About Us

The Career Development Centre at VIT plays a significant role in the school's successful placement record. The center manages the logistics of placement activities on campus and provides training opportunities for students to help them secure jobs with both national and international companies. There is also a dedicated Placement and Training unit within the Career Development Centre that focuses on developing students' skills and aptitudes.

The VIT placement office is dedicated to providing students with a variety of opportunities to secure employment with companies of their choice after graduation. They do this by organizing meetings with professional trainers and alumni, as well as helping students prepare for interviews and group discussions. The Career Development Centre also ensures that students are proficient in coding and aptitude skills to help them perform well in tests and interviews.

Recruiting companies visit the campus in July, and the placement process begins with a wide range of job opportunities. Many students receive offers from top companies, including Super Dream Offer and Dream Offer.
